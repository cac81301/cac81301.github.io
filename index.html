BREAKING NEWS: THE RETURN OF ELVIS

On August 16, 1977, the world was shocked as they heard that the "King of Rock" Elvis Presley passed away from a heart complication at his home Graceland in Memphis, Tennessee. From this day forward, the rock world forever felt the impact of the loss of one of the world's greatest musicians.

With his memory still ingrained in the music world, children, grandparents, teachers and friends are now able to embrace the life and the music career of Elvis through a brand new show in Los Angeles, coming this fall to the Hollywood Bowl. For three nights only, Elvis will return to the stage to perform your favorite hits, from Jailhouse Rock to Burning Love !

The live show will take place August 15, August 16 and August 17 to commerate 40 years since the passing of the King. Tickets will go on sale to Visa cardholders February 2, 2017 and will be available to the general public on February 5, 2017. During the month of March, we will run a platinum special, offering a three-course meal during the show for only $30 more.

America, grab your blue suede shoes for a show you won't want to miss!
